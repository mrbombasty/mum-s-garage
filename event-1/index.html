The Startup Series

Entrepreneurs Unleashed is a free monthly event designed to encourage entrepreneurship in Auckland.

At each event we have 2-3 experienced entrepreneurs and/or startup specialists share their stories and hacks for starting a business. It’s a fun environment with lots of opportunities to ask questions, discuss ideas and meet other entrepreneurs.
