Mum's Garage partners with ColabNZ

We’re excited to announce a partnership arrangement with ColabNZ, a shared workspace for startups and small businesses in Auckland.

It’s a big step up from the garage... With epic views over the city and a boardroom with a table tennis table! The culture and vibe of ColabNZ aligns perfectly with what we are trying to create through Mum’s Garage. It's place where people can be surrounded by like-minded individuals who are doing awesome things, hustling hard, chasing their passions and building epic startups.

We are excited to offer the Mum’s Garage community a deal of $250/month for a desk (this includes internet!), down from $400! It’s a perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in an environment of high performers and take your business development to the next level. Spots are limited so enquire now if you’re interested so you don’t miss out!
