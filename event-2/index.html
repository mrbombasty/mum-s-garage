about the event

The Startup Series is broken into the following 4 2hr workshops.

Workshop 1: Mindset

“80 percent of success is due to psychology—mindset, beliefs, and emotions—and only 20 percent is due to strategy—the specific steps needed to accomplish a result” Anthony Robbins

In this workshop you will be introduced to the key concepts behind developing a mindset for success as an entrepreneur.

Workshop 2: Your Startup's DNA

We will work through the passion, vision, values and story behind your idea. This will help you to build a strong platform to move forward with your business.

Workshop 3: Customer

To be successful in businesses you need to know your customer better than they know themselves! We'll cover what you need to know, how to find it out and how to build relationships with your customers so they help to solve your problems for you.

Workshop 4: Product Development

Learn how to build a product or service that people want without a lot of initial investment (money and time).
