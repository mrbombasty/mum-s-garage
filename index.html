Let's chat

Our mission is to introduce you to entrepreneurship and help you be successful in your first year of starting a business

Starting a business is hard when you've never done it before. School, university and social conditioning prepares us for employment not entrepreneurship. Most of us haven't been taught how to build a startup, which it's hard to turn that idea into something and get through the first year of starting.



ABOUT US

We are the home for budding entrepreneurs in Auckland who want to turn ideas into businesses

Our workshops, events and community activities are all designed to provide you with the advice and support you need to be successful in the first year of starting a business.



WHAT WE OFFER

We think you'll be a great fit if...

You've got a bone to pick with the world and you want to do something to resolve it.


You're stuck in a soul sucking corporate job and you've finally woken up and realised you'll be miserable if you're doing the same thing in 5 years


You're at university because that's the so called path to success, but you actually want to make some serious impact on the world.


You want to drive large-scale positive change and you know the best way to do this is through business.

What we do best

Startup Education

Non-intimidating, founder friendly workshops and events to help you learn the ropes for starting a business

We teach a human-centred, simple approach to starting a business. We care about developing well-rounded founders who can go on to produce great companies.

Community

Helping you to find a tribe of people with similar beliefs and ambitions.

You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with. It's important you surround yourself with other on a similar mission.

If you have a good community, then generally you can find someone who can help with general and/or domain specific knowledge.

Collaboration

Working with others to cover the bases needed to help you along your startup journey

We've partnered with others who have similar values and beliefs when it comes to business, to help plug gaps in the startup development journey. Check out some of the people we work with below.
